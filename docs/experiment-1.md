## Experiment 1

As an initial experiment we are decoupling the ssr implementation from the AngularNodeAppEngine and in general from 
the AppEngine implementation.

We are decoupling it by reimplementing its internal parts and trying to get a better idea of what we are loosing by doing this.

The experiment results are visible in the src/exp-1 directory and can be enabled with the OOS_EXPERIMENT=1 env
